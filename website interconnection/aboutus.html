About the Hotel

Over four generations, the same family has built up and developed Hotel Continental into one of Norway’s finest five-star hotels, and a member of the Leading Hotels of the World. In addition to our 151 rooms and suites, we offer five restaurants and bars with world-class food and drinks as well as a number of meeting and function rooms. Visitors from all over the world and local regulars create a lively and international ambience. Our unique art collection, history and atmosphere help to ensure that a stay with us becomes a memorable five-star experience. Hotel Continental is a natural choice for overnight accommodation in central Oslo. Businesses large and small use us for their conferences and meetings, and many private individuals celebrate anniversaries and other occasions here. We provide a five-star service at all times in order to retain our position as Oslo’s finest luxury hotel. Hotel Continental’s vision is “to be one of the stars that make Continental shine”.
